Beit Jala Garlic Sauce

Description

This is apparently Ariel Sharon's favourite dish from Bayt Jala's Qabar Restauraunt.

Ingredients

  • olive oil (EVOO)
  • potato (likely russet)
  • garlic cloves (young and decored)
  • lemons (fresh)
  1. Scrupulously peel enough cloves of garlic to half-fill your blender.
  2. Top with an equally scrupulously peeled and boiled white potato.
  3. Add the juice of one fat lemon.
  4. Add to that enough olive oil to mostly cover the solid ingredients, and whip it as if you were making a mayonnaise, until it has become a snow-white emulsified mass with the texture of whipped cream.
  5. Serve with roasted meats, such as spatchcocked chicken.
